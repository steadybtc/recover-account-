Recover Your Hacked or Disabled Account

Get your account back safely and quickly.

If your Facebook or other account was hacked or disabled, it can often be recovered

Losing access to your social account is alarming — but in most cases, accounts that were hacked, taken over, or disabled can be restored if the right steps are taken quickly. Follow these important steps to secure your data and begin recovery.

Recommended steps:
  1. Secure your email & phone: Ensure the contact details tied to the account are still accessible.
  2. Use official recovery pages: Visit the platform’s help center for verified recovery links.
  3. Gather proof: Collect evidence like screenshots or IDs if required.
  4. Change passwords: Update all linked accounts and enable two-factor authentication.
